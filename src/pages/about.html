Our Story

At Jeff Tuition, we believe in the potential of every child. We empower students with a deep-rooted passion for learning and help them achieve their full potential. Our teachers hold themselves accountable to make each and every lesson a solution-driven experience by keeping track of all student progress on individualized plans, while providing targeted one-on-one interventions as needed.

Our tutors are experts at teaching. With our comprehensive curriculum and easy-to-follow lesson plans, your child will be on the path to success in no time. We use games and quizzes to make learning fun, even for those who hate doing their homework.

At the end of the day, our mission is simple -- to help every child achieve their full potential. Join us on this exciting journey of learning and discovery!

Our expert teachers will guide your child to success in all school subjects and help them discover their true passion. It’s never too late or too early to start planning for your child’s future.

Fiona Wang Founder
